About the Department

The Mechanical Engineering (ME) Department offers a comprehensive 3-year diploma course focusing on design, manufacturing, thermal science, and automation. The curriculum is AICTE-approved and provides students with both theoretical foundation and practical industrial knowledge.

Key Subjects

  • Engineering Mechanics
  • Thermodynamics
  • Fluid Mechanics
  • Manufacturing Technology
  • Machine Design
  • CAD/CAM
