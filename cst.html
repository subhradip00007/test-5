About the Department

The Computer Science and Technology (CST) Department offers a modern 3-year diploma course focused on programming, systems, and software engineering. It is approved by AICTE and designed to equip students with practical and theoretical knowledge in the field of computer science.

Key Subjects

  • Programming in C, C++, Python
  • Operating Systems
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Database Management Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Web Development
